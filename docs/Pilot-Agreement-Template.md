## 1. PURPOSE

The Partner Organization expresses its intent to participate in a **structured pilot deployment** of the LitSecure Sentinel platform, a national-grade cybersecurity incident management and intelligence platform designed for Malawi.

This Letter of Intent is non-binding and serves to establish shared commitment to exploring a formal operational relationship.

---

## 3. PILOT OBJECTIVES

During the pilot, both parties agree to evaluate:

1. **Incident Reporting Efficiency** — reduction in time-to-report vs current process
2. **Threat Detection Accuracy** — AI classification quality against known incidents
3. **Analyst Productivity** — cases managed per analyst per week
4. **Integration Capability** — ability to connect to existing tools
5. **User Adoption** — platform engagement rate among designated users

---

## 4. PARTNER COMMITMENTS

The Partner Organization agrees to:

- Assign a **Technical Liaison** to coordinate the deployment
- Provide **2–5 analyst accounts** for active use during the pilot
- Submit **weekly feedback** via the built-in AI Learning panel
- Attend **3 check-in meetings** (Week 2, Week 6, Week 10)
- Provide a **written evaluation** at the end of the 90-day period

---

## 5. LITSECURE COMMITMENTS

LitSecure Systems agrees to:

- Provide a **fully configured instance** within 10 business days of agreement
- Deliver **onboarding training** (2-hour session, in-person or virtual)
- Maintain **99% uptime** during business hours (Mon–Fri, 08:00–17:00 CAT)
- Respond to support requests within **4 business hours**
- Keep all partner data **confidential and isolated**

---

## 6. DATA PRIVACY

All incident data, investigation records, and user activity during the pilot shall:
- Be stored exclusively within Malawi or on partner-approved infrastructure
- Not be shared with any third party without written consent
- Be deleted or transferred to the partner upon pilot conclusion if not renewed
- Be protected in compliance with the **Malawi Electronic Transactions and Cyber Security Act (ETCSA) 2016** and the **Data Protection Act 2024**
