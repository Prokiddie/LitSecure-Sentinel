## Key Assumptions

1. USD/MWK rate: 1,730 (June 2026 estimate)
2. Annual churn: 10% (government contracts typically renew)
3. Sales cycle: 3–6 months for government, 1–3 months for NGOs
4. Gross margin: 75–80% (primarily staff and API costs)
5. MACRA pilot converts to full contract in Q1 2027
6. SADC expansion requires 1 in-country partner per market
